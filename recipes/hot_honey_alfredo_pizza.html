Hot Honey Alfredo Pizza

Description

Sweet and Spicy and Creamy pizza at your service!

Ingredients

  • Any preferred premade pizza crust
  • Any preferred alfredo sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Spinach or Arugula
  • Mike's Hot Honey
  • Italian seasoning, parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes
  • Mozzarella cheese

    • Steps

    1. Prepare crust on an oven sheet
    2. One ladle of alfredo sauce in center of the crust and in a circular motion, spread evenly
    3. Add seasonings of choice onto sauce
    4. Add a generous amount of mozzarella cheese onto the pizza crust
    5. Layer pepperoni and veggies
    6. Add some olive oil and season veggies
    7. Bake or airfry at 360 degrees until cheese is melted, then set to 400 until crust is golden brown
    8. Layer Mike's Hot Honey to finish and enjoy!
