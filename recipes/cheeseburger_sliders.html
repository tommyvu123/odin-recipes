Cheeseburger Sliders

Description

Savory and cheesy goodness to satisfy your munchies!

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground meat (80/20)
  • Half a white onion
  • King Hawaiian buns
  • Choice of Meat Seasoning: Garlic Powder, Paprika, Salt/Pepper, Onion Powder
  • Nacho cheese and American Cheese
  • I Can't Believe It's Not Butter!Â®
  • Optional: Lettuce + Tomatoes

    • Steps

    1. Cut King Hawaiian buns in half top and bottom
    2. Dice your onions and set aside
    3. Cook ground meat on medium-high heat until almost cooked
    4. Add preferred meat seasoning
    5. Remove ground meat and strain.
    6. Add in diced onions, season with same seasoning and add back in ground meat
    7. Stir in nacho cheese sauce and cook for an additional 2 minutes
    8. Add meat to buns and get ready to bake
    9. Add on cheese slices
    10. Bake in oven on 360 for 8 minutes or until toasted
    11. Good to go!
